About Us

At Canddy Vee, we believe in creating warmth one stitch at a time. Inspired by tradition and crafted with love, our handmade crochet items are designed to bring comfort, beauty, and a personal touch to your everyday life. From cozy hats to charming scrunchies, every piece tells a story — woven with care, patience, and passion.

We’re a family-rooted business, passing down the art of crochet through generations. Whether you're gifting a loved one or treating yourself, we’re honored to be part of your journey. Thank you for supporting handmade. 💖 — With love, Vuyokazi Canddy Mjacu
